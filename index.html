Common Questions

01. What level of care do you provide?

We provide comprehensive care ranging from assistance with daily living activities (bathing, dressing) to specialized medical monitoring, medication administration, and memory care for residents with dementia.

02. Are meals provided?

Yes, we prepare three nutritious, home-cooked meals daily, plus healthy snacks. We accommodate specific dietary restrictions such as diabetic, low-sodium, and texture-modified diets.

03. What are the visiting hours?

We maintain an open-door policy for families. While we encourage visits between 9 AM and 7 PM to respect residents' rest schedules, we are flexible to accommodate family needs.

04. Is the facility staffed 24/7?

Absolutely. We have awake staff present 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. An RN is also on call at all times to address any medical concerns that may arise.

05. Do you offer respite care?

Yes, we offer short-term respite stays depending on bed availability. This is an excellent option for families needing a temporary break or for post-hospitalization recovery.
